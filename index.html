Raphaël Pagès

I am currently doing a postdoc at the Institute of Algebra in Johannes Kepler Universität (JKU, Linz, Austria) with Manuel Kauers.

I have a thesis in mathematics on the factorisation of linear differential operators in positive characteristic. I carried out my PhD at the University of
Bordeaux, under the joint supervision of Alin Bostan and Xavier Caruso between September 2020 and February 2024 in the Project team CANARI and INRIA team MathExp.

Email: Raphael.Pages at jku.at



Research interests:

  • Differential operators
  • Algebraic curves
  • Central simple and Azumaya algebras
  • Finite fields and fields of positive characteristic
  • D-Algebraic Functions
  • Symbolic Computation
